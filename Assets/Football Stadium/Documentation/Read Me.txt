Documentation of 3D Football/Soccer Stadium

In this Pack, We have seen 3D model of Football Stadium. Low poly 3D stadium you can use it in your games and videos as well.

Models are made with 4 textures. Models are low poly. The Complete 3D 3D Football Stadium consists of different objects created individually, 
so you can use them to create an intuitive and eye-catching user interface for your 3D game or animated video. The vectors can be edited 
and placed at any scene in the game/video to give it a more defined look. Specially this pack is use for 3D Football game. 
we have different folders:

1) Material:
	Models are made more than 6 materials.
2) Models: 
	In this models folder you will see Model of 3D Football Stadium inside it.
3) Prefabs: 
	 you will see only prefab of this model.
4) Scene: 
	There you will see all the props in the scene.
5) Texture:
	Models are made more than 4 textures.
6) Documentation
	Read me file


All the prefabs are created, you can use each prefabs according to your game environment. you just need to drag and drop each prefab
wherever you want. you can create a big environment for your 3d games and 3d animation. Each objects are scaleable, you can transform 
and rotate every object.
